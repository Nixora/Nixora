OUR APPROACH

Tech-driven and
human-centered

Our sustainability standards promote transparency and responsibility in how we operate in areas like environmental awareness, social responsibility and corporate governance. At Nixora, these pillars play a crucial role in the way we create value for our stakeholders and the communities we belong to.

As a software development company, we're coding the future, while caring for the present. Our teams are committed to protecting the environment, promoting transparent innovations and empowering local communities, because by taking action now, we can shape a better future for everyone.

THE THREE PILLARS

Our ESG framework

Environmental

We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and operating in an environmentally responsible way. From green data centers to paperless workflows, we embed sustainability into every layer of our operations.

  • Carbon footprint reduction targets
  • Renewable energy in our offices
  • Paperless & digital-first operations
  • Green software engineering practices

Social

People are at the heart of everything we do. We invest in our employees, support local communities and champion diversity and inclusion across our global workforce.

  • Diversity & inclusion programs
  • Community tech education initiatives
  • Employee wellbeing & mental health
  • Equal pay & fair hiring practices

Governance

We uphold the highest standards of corporate governance, ensuring transparency, accountability and ethical conduct in all our business activities and client relationships.

  • ISO 27001 information security
  • Transparent reporting & audits
  • Anti-corruption & ethics policy
  • Data privacy & GDPR compliance
