OUR STORY

From boutique software house to
global technology partner

In 2019, two ambitious developers wanted to build interesting business apps for international markets using emerging technologies. Over 7 years later, the company they founded does just that and so much more.

As a trusted software development and consultancy company for organizations around the world and across sectors, we provide our clients with the expertise, resources and product engineering they need to drive their business.

A culture that prioritizes openness, ownership and respect encourages our global team to crave more, act as true partners and deliver meaningful value.

Through organic growth, external investment and strategic acquisitions, we've gone from a boutique software house to a digital transformation partner with a presence throughout Europe, the US and Latin America. Our path has been shaped by many of the same challenges as our clients entering new markets, developing domain knowledge, integrating new technologies. It's why we're able to guide digital accelerations and engineer software that impacts today and inspires tomorrow.
