The Opportunity

Turning sports data and player engagement into scalable betting products

The global sports betting market is growing rapidly driven by mobile-first players, live in-play betting, and the expansion of regulated markets across the US, Europe, and beyond. Operators who win are those who can deliver the fastest odds, the most engaging player experiences, and the most robust risk management all while staying compliant with an increasingly complex regulatory landscape. We help sportsbooks, white-label platform providers, and iGaming operators build the software they need to compete: fast, scalable, compliant, and built for the demands of real-time sports data.

What We Build

End-to-end sports betting software solutions

Sportsbook & Odds Engine

We build high-performance sportsbook platforms and real-time odds engines integrating with data providers, processing millions of events per second, and delivering sub-50ms odds updates for live in-play betting across all major sports.

Risk Management & Trading Systems

We develop automated risk management platforms, liability monitoring tools, and trading systems that give operators real-time visibility and control over exposure protecting margins while maximizing betting volume.

Player-Facing Apps & Web Platforms

We design and build engaging, high-performance betting apps and web platforms with live streaming integration, bet builders, cash-out functionality, and personalized promotions optimized for conversion and retention.

Payments & Wallet Systems

We build secure, multi-currency payment platforms and digital wallet systems integrating with payment processors, e-wallets, and banking APIs to deliver fast deposits, instant withdrawals, and full AML/KYC compliance.

Player Analytics & CRM Platforms

Responsible Gambling & Compliance Tools

We build responsible gambling systems including self-exclusion, deposit limits, reality checks, and AI-powered problem gambling detection ensuring operators meet regulatory requirements and protect their players.
