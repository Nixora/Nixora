WHY SOFTWARE AUDITING

Software auditing services that improve quality & reduce risk

A software audit gives you an independent, expert view of your codebase, architecture, and development processes identifying hidden risks, technical debt, security vulnerabilities, and compliance gaps before they become costly problems.

Whether you're preparing for a major release, evaluating an acquisition, onboarding a new development team, or simply want confidence in your software quality, our audits deliver actionable insights with clear remediation priorities.

WHAT WE AUDIT

Comprehensive software audit services

Code Quality Audit

We review your codebase for maintainability, readability, duplication, complexity, and adherence to coding standards identifying technical debt and providing a prioritized refactoring roadmap.

Architecture Review

We assess your system architecture for scalability, resilience, and alignment with business requirements identifying design flaws, single points of failure, and opportunities for modernization.

Security Audit

We identify vulnerabilities in your code, dependencies, and infrastructure covering OWASP Top 10, injection flaws, authentication weaknesses, and data exposure risks with a detailed remediation plan.

Performance Audit

We profile your application to identify bottlenecks, memory leaks, inefficient queries, and scalability limits providing concrete optimizations that improve response times and reduce infrastructure costs.

Process & SDLC Audit

We evaluate your software development lifecycle reviewing planning, testing, deployment, and documentation practices to identify inefficiencies and recommend process improvements.

Compliance Audit

We verify your software meets relevant regulatory and industry standards GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, ISO 27001, SOC 2 providing a compliance gap analysis and remediation guidance.

OUR PROCESS

From audit request to
actionable report

Every audit follows a structured, transparent process so you always know what we're doing, why, and what to expect at each stage.

01

Scoping & Kickoff

We define the audit scope, gather access to your codebase, documentation, and systems, and align on objectives and deliverables with your team.

02

Deep Analysis

Our engineers conduct a thorough review using automated tools and manual expert analysis examining code, architecture, security, performance, and processes.

03

Findings & Prioritization

We compile all findings, assess severity and business impact, and prioritize issues into a clear remediation roadmap critical, high, medium, and low.

04

Report & Presentation

We deliver a comprehensive written report and present findings to your team explaining every issue, its impact, and the recommended fix in plain language.

05

Remediation Support

We remain available to support your team during remediation answering questions, reviewing fixes, and conducting a follow-up verification audit if needed.

Common questions about software auditing

Common triggers include: before a major release, when onboarding a new development team, before or after an acquisition, when experiencing recurring bugs or performance issues, when preparing for compliance certification, or simply as a regular health check every 12-18 months.

A focused audit (e.g., security-only or code quality for a single service) typically takes 1-2 weeks. A comprehensive audit covering code, architecture, security, performance, and processes for a large system usually takes 3-4 weeks. We provide a timeline estimate after the initial scoping call.

We typically need read-only access to your source code repository, architecture documentation, and relevant system environments. We sign NDAs before any access is granted and follow strict data handling protocols throughout the engagement.

Our reports include an executive summary, detailed findings with severity ratings, business impact assessments, specific remediation recommendations for each issue, a prioritized action plan, and an overall health score. We also present findings in a live session with your team.
