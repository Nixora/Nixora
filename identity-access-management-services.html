WHAT WE OFFER

End-to-end identity and access management services

Identity Management Systems

We design and implement enterprise identity management systems that centralize user lifecycle management from onboarding and role assignment to offboarding across all your applications and environments.

Single Sign-On (SSO)

We implement SSO solutions that let users authenticate once and access all authorized applications seamlessly improving user experience while reducing password fatigue and help desk overhead.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

We deploy adaptive MFA solutions that add a critical second layer of security balancing strong protection with a frictionless user experience across web, mobile, and enterprise applications.

Privileged Access Management (PAM)

We implement PAM solutions that control, monitor, and audit privileged account access eliminating the most dangerous attack vector in enterprise environments with zero-trust principles.

Zero Trust Architecture

We design and implement Zero Trust security frameworks that verify every user, device, and request eliminating implicit trust and reducing the blast radius of any potential breach.

IAM Security Audits

We conduct comprehensive IAM security audits reviewing access policies, identifying over-privileged accounts, detecting orphaned identities, and providing a prioritized remediation roadmap.

FAQ

Common questions about IAM

IAM is the framework of policies, processes, and technologies that ensures the right people have the right access to the right resources at the right time. It covers user authentication, authorization, role management, single sign-on, multi-factor authentication, and privileged access management.

IAM manages the identities and access rights of all users across your organization. PAM (Privileged Access Management) is a subset of IAM focused specifically on controlling and monitoring accounts with elevated privileges such as system administrators and database admins which represent the highest security risk.

A focused IAM project such as deploying SSO or MFA can be completed in 4�8 weeks. A full enterprise IAM implementation covering identity governance, PAM, and Zero Trust typically takes 3�6 months in phased sprints. We always start with an identity audit to scope the work accurately.
