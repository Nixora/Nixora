FAQ

Common questions about generative AI

Can't find your answer? Contact us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

Traditional ML models are trained to classify, predict, or detect patterns in data. Generative AI models (like LLMs) can create new content text, code, images, summaries based on natural language instructions. They're far more flexible and require less labeled training data for many use cases.

Data security is a top priority. We can deploy AI solutions using private cloud instances, on-premise models, or enterprise API agreements that prevent your data from being used for model training. We also implement data anonymization and access controls throughout the pipeline.

We use RAG architectures to ground AI responses in verified data sources, implement output validation layers, add confidence scoring, and design human-in-the-loop workflows for high-stakes decisions. We also continuously monitor outputs and retrain or adjust prompts based on real-world performance.

A focused AI feature or tool can go from concept to production in 6�10 weeks. A more complex system with custom fine-tuning, RAG pipelines, and enterprise integrations typically takes 3�5 months. We always start with a 2-week discovery and prototype phase to validate feasibility before full build.
