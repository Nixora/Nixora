What We Build

End-to-end telecom software solutions

BSS/OSS Modernization

We modernize legacy billing, mediation, and operations support systems — migrating monolithic BSS/OSS stacks to cloud-native, microservices-based architectures that reduce costs, improve agility, and support new business models.

5G & Network Management Platforms

We build network management systems, service orchestration platforms, and 5G core software components — enabling operators to automate network operations, reduce OPEX, and accelerate 5G service delivery.

Customer Experience & Self-Service

We design and build digital customer portals, self-service apps, and omnichannel CRM integrations — reducing churn, improving NPS, and cutting contact centre costs through better digital experiences.

AI-Powered Network Analytics

We build AI and ML platforms for network performance monitoring, predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, and capacity planning — helping operators move from reactive to proactive network management.

Cloud & Edge Computing Solutions

We help telecom operators leverage their network infrastructure for cloud and edge computing services — building platforms for multi-access edge computing (MEC), private 5G, and enterprise connectivity products.

Telecom Security & Fraud Management

We build fraud detection systems, signalling security platforms, and network security monitoring tools — protecting operators from revenue leakage, SS7/Diameter attacks, and SIM-swap fraud at scale.
