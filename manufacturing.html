What We Build

End-to-end manufacturing software solutions

Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES)

We build and integrate MES platforms that connect shop floor operations with enterprise systems — providing real-time production visibility, work order management, quality control, and OEE tracking across your entire manufacturing network.

Predictive Maintenance & Asset Management

We build IoT-connected predictive maintenance platforms that monitor equipment health in real time — using machine learning to detect anomalies, predict failures before they occur, and schedule maintenance to minimize production disruption.

Supply Chain & Logistics Platforms

We develop supply chain visibility platforms, demand forecasting tools, and logistics management systems — giving manufacturers end-to-end visibility across suppliers, inventory, and distribution to reduce costs and improve resilience.

Digital Twin & Simulation Platforms

We build digital twin platforms that create virtual replicas of production lines, equipment, and facilities — enabling manufacturers to simulate process changes, optimize layouts, and test new configurations without disrupting live operations.

Quality Management Systems (QMS)

We develop quality management platforms, automated inspection tools, and defect tracking systems — integrating with production data to identify quality issues at source, reduce scrap rates, and maintain compliance with industry standards.

Industrial IoT & Analytics Platforms

We build IIoT data platforms that collect, process, and analyze sensor data from machines, production lines, and facilities — delivering the operational intelligence manufacturers need to optimize throughput, energy use, and product quality.
