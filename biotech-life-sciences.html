What We Build

End-to-end life sciences software solutions

R&D Data Platforms

We build laboratory information management systems (LIMS), electronic lab notebooks (ELN), and research data platforms that centralize experimental data, accelerate analysis, and improve reproducibility across your R&D organization.

Clinical Trial Management

We develop clinical trial management systems (CTMS), eClinical platforms, and patient data collection tools — streamlining trial operations, improving data integrity, and accelerating time to regulatory submission.

Digital Health & Patient Platforms

We design and build patient-facing apps, remote monitoring platforms, and connected health solutions — enabling continuous care, improving patient engagement, and generating real-world evidence at scale.

Bioinformatics & AI/ML Solutions

We build AI-powered drug discovery tools, genomic data analysis pipelines, and predictive models that help researchers identify targets, predict outcomes, and accelerate the path from molecule to market.

Regulatory & Compliance Systems

We build regulatory submission tools, quality management systems (QMS), and audit trail platforms that keep you compliant with FDA 21 CFR Part 11, EU Annex 11, HIPAA, and GDPR requirements.

Manufacturing & Supply Chain Software

We develop manufacturing execution systems (MES), batch record management tools, and supply chain platforms for pharmaceutical and biotech manufacturers — ensuring traceability, quality, and GMP compliance.
