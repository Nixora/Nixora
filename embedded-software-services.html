WHAT WE OFFER

Full-cycle embedded software capabilities

Firmware Development

We design and develop robust firmware for microcontrollers and microprocessors, ensuring reliable low-level control of hardware components.

Hardware Platform Configuration

Our engineers configure and optimize hardware platforms from BSP bring-up to driver development tailored to your product requirements.

Embedded Application Development

We build embedded applications that run efficiently on constrained devices, from real-time control systems to IoT edge applications.

Verification & Testing

Comprehensive verification and validation services including unit testing, integration testing, HIL testing, and compliance testing for safety-critical systems.

RTOS & Embedded Linux

We develop and port real-time operating systems and embedded Linux distributions, including kernel customization and device tree configuration.

Embedded Consulting & Support

Expert consulting on architecture decisions, technology selection, and long-term firmware maintenance to keep your embedded systems running reliably.
